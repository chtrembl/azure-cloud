# Alternate Supplier Qualification and Approval Policy

**Policy Number:** SCM-POL-007
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Owner:** Director, Strategic Sourcing
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the process for qualifying and approving alternate suppliers to ensure continuity of supply while maintaining quality and compliance standards.

## 2. Approved Vendor List (AVL)

The Approved Vendor List is maintained by Strategic Sourcing and includes:
- Primary suppliers for each product category
- Pre-qualified alternate suppliers with validated capability
- Emergency-qualified suppliers with conditional approval

## 3. Standard Qualification Process

**Timeline:** 30–60 business days

1. Supplier capability assessment
2. Quality system audit (ISO 9001 minimum)
3. Sample production run and first article inspection
4. Commercial terms negotiation
5. Legal review and contract execution
6. System setup and testing

## 3.1 Emergency Qualification

**Timeline:** 48 hours

For stockout emergencies, pre-qualified alternate suppliers from the AVL may be activated under these conditions:

1. **Director approval** from Supply Chain Operations or Strategic Sourcing
2. **Quality Engineering sign-off** based on review of the alternate supplier's last 3 lot inspection records
3. **Documented business justification** including stockout impact assessment
4. Initial order limited to **50% of standard order quantity** until full qualification is complete
5. 100% incoming inspection for first 3 shipments from alternate source

## 4. Dual-Sourcing Authorization

When triggered by Supplier Escalation Runbook Tier 3:
- Dual-sourcing may proceed **without reducing** the minimum volume commitment to the primary supplier for **180 days**
- After 180 days, volume reallocation requires VP Supply Chain approval
- Dual-sourcing cost differential absorbed by Supply Chain Operations budget

## 5. Decision Authority

| Action | Authority |
|---|---|
| Add to AVL (standard) | Director, Strategic Sourcing |
| Emergency activation from AVL | Director, Supply Chain Operations |
| Dual-sourcing initiation | VP Supply Chain (per Escalation Runbook Tier 3) |
| Primary supplier replacement | SVP Operations + Legal |
