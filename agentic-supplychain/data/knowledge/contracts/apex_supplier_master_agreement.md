# Apex Manufacturing – Master Supply Agreement

**Parties:** Contoso Operations Inc. ("Buyer") and Apex Manufacturing Corp. ("Supplier")
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Term:** 3 years with annual renewal option

---

## 1. Scope of Supply

Apex Manufacturing shall supply the following product categories:
- Industrial Valve Assemblies (PRD-110)
- Hydraulic Pump Units (PRD-111)
- Custom mechanical components per engineering specifications

## 2. Pricing and Payment

- **Payment terms:** Net 30 from date of invoice
- **Annual price review:** Permitted once per calendar year with 90-day notice
- **Volume commitment:** Minimum $500,000 annual purchase volume

## 3. Delivery Terms

- **Standard lead time:** 21 calendar days from confirmed purchase order
- **Delivery location:** FOB destination to Buyer's designated warehouse
- **Delivery window:** ±2 business days from confirmed delivery date

## 4. Performance Standards

### 4.1 On-Time Delivery

- Target on-time delivery rate: **90% or higher** per rolling quarter
- Measurement: Shipment arriving within the confirmed delivery window

### 4.2 Delivery Penalties

- Shipments exceeding **10 calendar days** past the confirmed delivery date incur a penalty of **2% of the purchase order value per day**, capped at **20% of order value**.
- Penalties are applied as credits against future invoices.
- The Buyer must provide written notice of penalty assessment within 15 business days of delivery.

### 4.3 Chronic Non-Performance

- If the Supplier's on-time delivery rate falls below **50%** over any **rolling 90-day period**, the Buyer may:
  1. Issue a formal performance improvement notice
  2. Require a written corrective action plan within **10 business days**
  3. Escalate to **VP Supply Chain** review within **5 business days** of the 90-day threshold breach
  4. Initiate qualification of alternate suppliers under Section 8

## 5. Quality Standards

- Incoming inspection acceptance rate: **98%** minimum
- Defective units shall be replaced at Supplier's expense within 10 business days
- Three consecutive failed batch inspections trigger quality escalation per Section 4.3

## 6. Force Majeure

Standard force majeure clause applies. Supplier must notify Buyer within 48 hours and provide estimated recovery timeline.

## 7. Termination

- Either party may terminate with **90-day written notice**
- Immediate termination permitted for material breach unremedied within 30 days
- Chronic non-performance under Section 4.3 constitutes grounds for immediate termination

## 8. Alternate Supplier Rights

- Buyer retains the right to qualify and activate alternate suppliers at any time
- Upon triggering Section 4.3, Buyer may dual-source without reducing minimum volume commitment for 180 days
